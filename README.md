## Most Popular Breakfast Items

### Egg McMuffin  
A fresh egg, melted cheese, and Canadian style meat served on a warm English muffin.

### Sausage McGriddles  
A sweet and savory choice that combines a sausage patty with soft griddle cakes made with maple flavor.

### Hash Browns  
Golden and crispy potato patties that are served hot and enjoyed by almost every breakfast fan.

### Hotcakes  
Soft and fluffy pancakes served with butter and syrup that make a warm morning treat.
