Top Morning Choices

McDonald's serves a wide selection of warm and freshly prepared breakfast options. Many customers look for items such as the Egg McMuffin, Sausage McMuffin, Bacon Egg and Cheese Biscuit, Sausage Burrito, and classic Hotcakes. These items are among the most searched and most enjoyed breakfast options across the country.

Egg McMuffin

Made with a fresh cracked egg, American cheese, and Canadian style meat on a toasted English muffin.

Hash Browns

Crispy, golden, and one of the highest trending McDonald's breakfast items searched online every morning.

Sausage McGriddles

A sweet and savory combination of maple flavored griddle cakes and a sausage patty, loved for its unique taste.
